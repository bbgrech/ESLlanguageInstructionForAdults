Quick Level Test

Quick level test:

Please answer these questions prior to our first meeting. There are no right-or-wrong answers, however, a written response is also requested so that I may evaluate writing skills. (you may either copy and paste these into a word doc and then send them via email, or print them out and scan them to me).  Please do not use ‘spell-check’ or grammar corrections generated from the computer. Do not worry about making any sort of “mistake’😉 I like to tell my students that “mistakes are good, as long as we learn from them ;-):

  1. What is your primary reason for studying English?
  2. What subject area do you want to focus on? (speaking, writing, business, etc.
  3. What is the area of English which you find the most challenging (hardest) and why do you think that is so?
  4. Do you like studying English?
  5. What is your native language? Where did you grow up?
  6. What other languages do you speak? Are these native languages, or where/at what age/how long ago did you study these?
  7. For how long have you studied English?
  8. Where did you study and at what level? (country, level of school)
  9. What schedule are you hoping for? (1 hour per week/day, etc.)
  10. Do you have SKYPE? If not, can you install it and make it available?
  11. What are your hobbies and interests?
  12. Why did you choose to study with me?
  13. Other questions or comment?
