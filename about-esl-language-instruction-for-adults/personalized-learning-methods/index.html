Personalized Learning Methods

How are your Courses “Personalized”? 

Why I am the right teacher for you:

First, I understand first-hand that all relationships (including teacher-student) are give-and-take; They involve active give-and-take communication,  participation, and contributions between both the teacher and the student.  In other words, in any relationship both parties need to give-and-take in order for it to work.  In this way, I encourage student contributions to the learning, rather than simply having the teacher ‘control’ or ‘dictate’ everything.

Second, I cater each class to each individual student; No two-classes are alike.  My classes always start with a full-on interview with the student to make sure the student’s needs/desires are met.

Third, I guarantee* satisfaction.  *We evaluate each class as we go along, to make sure all parties are happy.  I also respect that not everyone is a perfect match, so mid-way through the determined schedule, we re-exam whether or not the student/teacher wishes to continue, no questions asked.  That means that there is no long-term commitment to the future classes, unless it’s so desired by both parties.  No more paying for unwanted classes, no more loss of time and money.  It’s not personal !

Forth:  The proof’s in the pudding !  Student ratings and reviews:  You can click here to read my past students’ evaluations. 

Bonus classes/Personalized Access:

Opportunities to join my club where you have continual access to a private teacher.

If, after completion of a class, any student wishes to join my club, he/she can have continual access to my private ‘spontaneous tutoring’ for a discounted club price.  This means that you can contact me, from anywhere in the world, and have assistance and/or any questions answered, whether it be grammar or just spontaneous help.  Your own personal source !

Professional classes which cater to adults. 

Because I also have a business background, I also specialize in classes such as : business writing, resume writing, speech writing/practice, and other business needs.

Culture: 

For “local” or “visiting students” (Malta), we also have cultural opportunities for private excursions (coming soon).  Also see our FAQ page for more details on this matter.

More about me:

Some of my other interests and passions include:  a profound respect for the environment and nature (all things organic), health and functional medicine topics, eating and living a healthy lifestyle, animal welfare (check out my other site: www.beesccc.com), culture, travel, reading, lifelong learning, building websites (I built my two sites myself !) and many more.  I love to “connect” with others who have similar interests !
